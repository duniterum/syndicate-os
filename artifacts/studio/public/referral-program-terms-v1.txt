THE SYNDICATE - MEMBER REFERRAL PROGRAM TERMS
Version: v1 (SYN.REFERRAL.MEMBER.TERMS.V1)
Canonical URL: https://thesyndicate.money/referral-program-terms-v1.txt
Program page:  https://thesyndicate.money/referral

These terms govern MEMBER_INTRODUCTION sources only. Other source
classes (partner, builder, affiliate) are governed by their own
versioned terms documents, each with its own recorded hash.

This document states the terms of The Syndicate member referral
program. Its keccak256 hash is recorded on-chain as the metadataHash
of member referral sources created under this program in the Source
Registry on Avalanche C-Chain (chain id 43114). Anyone can verify the
commitment: download this exact file and compute keccak256 over its
raw bytes.

1. THE PROGRAM
A member of The Syndicate may hold a personal referral link. When a
buyer joins The Syndicate through that link, the buyer's own purchase
transaction pays the referrer's commission directly, on-chain, before
the net protocol contribution is routed. The referrer is not paid from
Syndicate revenue after the fact. The referrer is paid from the
purchase transaction before the net protocol contribution is routed.

2. WHO IS ELIGIBLE TO REFER
- The referrer holds a Syndicate seat (is a member).
- The referrer's source exists in the on-chain Source Registry,
  created by a founder-signed transaction, and is ACTIVE at the
  moment of the referred purchase.
- The sale contract additionally requires the referrer's wallet to
  hold SYN at the moment of a referred purchase. Holding SYN pays
  nothing by itself; a commission compensates a completed
  introduction. A referrer wallet holding zero SYN at the moment of
  a purchase receives no commission on that purchase.

3. THE COMMISSION
- Starting rate: 5% (500 basis points) of the gross purchase amount.
- The applied rate and amount are always the sale contract's own
  computation at the moment of purchase. When the contract computes
  zero (for example: the source is paused or revoked, or an
  eligibility condition fails), the commission for that purchase
  is zero.
- Scope: LIFETIME attribution. Eligible purchases by the referred
  buyer, including repeat purchases, carry the commission.
- Caps: sources under this program version are created with no gross
  cap and no per-buyer cap. Future source versions may carry caps; a
  change of terms is never silent (see section 6).
- The rate may be raised over time under the Connector recognition
  ladder, up to the on-chain maximum for member introductions of 12%
  (1200 basis points). A rate change applies from the moment it is
  recorded on-chain; it is never retroactive.
- An acquired rate is not lowered for inactivity. A source may still
  be paused or revoked for cause (see section 6).

4. WHAT THE COMMISSION IS - AND IS NOT
A referral commission is a transparent payment for an eligible,
completed member introduction: payment for an act. It is not passive
income, not token yield, not a dividend, not equity, not a downline,
and not a profit promise. Referring never grants a better SYN price,
neither to the referrer nor to the buyer. Membership is not an
investment.

5. TAXES
A referral commission is taxable income for the referrer. The
referrer is solely responsible for reporting and paying any taxes
that apply to commissions received under this program.

6. PROGRAM ADMINISTRATION, PAUSE AND REVOCATION
- Sources are created, paused, reactivated, and revoked by
  founder-signed on-chain transactions in the Source Registry. Every
  such change emits a public on-chain event; there are no silent
  edits.
- The Syndicate may pause or revoke a source for abuse, fraud, or
  breach of these terms, and may pause the program as a whole.
- Anti-abuse rules: no self-referral (rejected by the contract
  itself), no circular referrals, no bots or fake accounts, no wash
  purchases, and no misleading promotion - including any presentation
  of membership as an investment or of commissions as passive income.
  Abnormal patterns are held for review.

7. THE MECHANICS, STATED HONESTLY
- The commission destination is the wallet recorded on-chain as the
  source's payment destination (the contract's payoutWallet field).
- Payment is pushed inside the buyer's transaction. If the push
  fails, the amount is held in escrow on-chain and can be released to
  the recorded destination wallet by anyone at any time.
- A buyer's first attributed purchase links the source to that buyer
  on-chain; the link then applies automatically to that buyer's later
  purchases.
- The buyer sees the referral and the commission before signing.

8. CHANGES TO THESE TERMS
These terms are versioned. A new version is published at a new URL
with a new hash, and newly created sources reference the new hash.
The version recorded in a source's metadataHash remains the version
governing that source until the source's terms are updated by a
founder-signed on-chain transaction.
