What makes this grade-AAA

The craft we took from the best — and the manipulation we refused.

✓
One-screen standing — your 4 figures, the link and the ladder all legible without a single drill-down (PartnerStack/Dub). Detail & terms collapse to the bottom.
✓
Settled vs owed, each labelled — “Commission paid” beside “Held in escrow”, so you never wonder why two numbers differ (Stripe Connect’s trust discipline).
✓
Verify on every figure — a cyan “verify on-chain ↗” beside the money. No number is asserted; each links its proof. The one thing no affiliate dashboard can match.
✓
The ladder is a road, never a leaderboard — your rung, your rate, the next threshold, Summit as a distant landmark. A count-progress, not a filling-money bar (Patreon removed theirs — it misled).
✕
No downline, ever — you never see who you introduced. Own-row only (ADR-003) — the hard line that separates us from MLM.
✕
No hype — no “earn up to X”, no projections, no countdowns, no confetti, no leaderboards. The money is shown openly; its legal shape is stated in the same breath.

  4. The money wording — “Commission paid” / “Held in escrow”, or “Paid to you” / “Owed to you (pending payout)”? Your pick, read on screen.
