S0 · Binder

The receipt binder

studiedStripe receiptsRainbow expanded txApple Wallet OrdersCoinbase / Wise / RevolutBaymard · NN/g · W3C WAI

Take the craft

  • List → detail: open on a scannable list; a row opens the full ticket.
  • Fixed receipt anatomy: what+status+time → reference № → itemization → proof, every time.
  • Status always current — reflects the latest chain truth, never a stale “success”.
  • Roll-up as a statement, not a scoreboard: “N receipts · total · since date”.

Reject the manipulation

  • Expiring receipts (Stripe links die in 30 days) — ours are permanent.
  • Email-as-receipt (“check your inbox”) — the in-app binder is the record.
  • Upsell on the receipt (“buy again”, promos) — it’s a record, not an ad.
  • “You spent X” spend-framing & buyer leaderboards.
→ Our version

Opens on your receipt list (gold only on the total). Each row → the permanent full ticket that used to vanish after checkout, ending in a proof block: the tx hash → Snowtrace, copy-hash, Save-as-PDF. No expiry, no email, no upsell.

S1 · Feed

The My | Protocol pulse lens

studiedGitHub activityLinear inboxZerion / Etherscansegmented-control specsNN/g empty states

Take the craft

  • Segmented control, not tabs: [ Mine | Protocol ] switch the lens on one shared grammar, in place.
  • Decode to human intent: “You were recognized”, not a stack of raw transfers.
  • Sticky day headers (Today / This week) + honest batching (“12 joined today”).
  • “N new” pill on demand — never a silent ticker that shifts your reading.

Reject the manipulation

  • Auto-inject “liveliness” & red-dot unread pressure for non-actionable events.
  • FOMO / scarcity (“X joined in the last hour — don’t miss out”).
  • Leaderboards / a directory of other members (GitHub-style who-did-what).
  • Number-go-up / PnL badges bolted onto rows.
→ Our version

One feed, a [Mine | Protocol] toggle (gold on the selected side). Mine = strictly your own row, behind the sign-in wall. Protocol = public events, but people de-identified (“A member joined” / “12 today”) with full contract facts — each line carrying its tx proof. A quiet “as of block N” stamp instead of a fake live ticker.

S2 · Histories

Per-row histories & detail

studiedMercury transactionsStripe payment detailsCoinbase historyNN/g data tablesCarbon statusBaymard sort/filter

Take the craft

  • Human label first (“Introduction”, “Commission received”) + date — not a raw hash.
  • Filter and sort, both visible (they’re distinct jobs).
  • Row → non-modal side drawer so the list stays visible and referenceable.
  • Status = colour + icon + text, legible in grayscale; detail is an event timeline.

Reject the manipulation

  • “Total earned” hero ticker crowning the ledger as growth.
  • Directory temptation: linking an introduced person’s row to their profile/downline.
  • Colour-only status & email-me-a-copy receipts.
  • Asserted figures with no proof (“you earned $X”, trust us).
→ Our version

Opens on your itemized record (“47 entries — every one verifiable”). Row = date · human label · amount (introductions carry a neutral non-cash marker, never $0) · status pill · Verify handle on the row. Row → a right drawer headlined by the proof + a “how this was computed” trace to the on-chain event. Pseudonymous handles only — never another member’s address.

The reusable output

The A1 honesty contract

Five invariants every activity, receipt and history surface renders the same way — so they read as one system. This is the checklist the first build of every A1 surface must pass, not a cleanup after you complain.

  1. 1
    Verify-first row
    Every figure carries a Verify ↗ to the explorer. A figure we can’t prove, we don’t show.
  2. 2
    Own-row only
    Never another member’s address, no leaderboard, ever (ADR-003). Aggregates shown as the true on-chain total with their own proof — never a roster of people.
  3. 3
    Anti-financialization skin
    Static, neutral amounts — no counters, green-pump, ROI/APY, streaks, countdowns, or confetti. Recognition lives in a visibly non-monetary register.
  4. 4
    Honest three states
    Loading (skeleton matching the real layout) · Empty (“nothing yet” + one true next step) · Error (what failed + retry). Pending stays Pending until the chain confirms.
  5. 5
    Accessible by construction
    Semantic table/feed, ARIA, keyboard reach, WCAG 2.2 AA in both themes, reduced-motion, absolute time always available.
