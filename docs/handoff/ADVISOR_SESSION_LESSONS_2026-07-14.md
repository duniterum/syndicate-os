   read; the native never feels talked down to. Standard crypto vocabulary stays natural;
   INTERNAL machinery jargon never user-facing except after the human words, for verifiers.
2. **VISUAL CHANGE LAW** — the founder is visual: founder-approved wireframe BEFORE code;
   PREVIEW GATE (real rendered page, his browser, desktop AND mobile, every asset loading)
   before ANY commit. A rejection costs nothing. A discussed wireframe is a CONTRACT —
   it enters the prompt described zone by zone.
3. **SYSTEM-FIRST LAW + MIRROR RULE** — before building "a part of X", the COMPLETE inventory
   of X (origin repos + deployed reality) goes on screen and the founder picks scope from the
   full picture. Building 3 lanes when the system has 15 classes is the named failure.
   MIRROR: the origin is a QUARRY, never a LAW — harvest its completeness and ambition,
   never its old constraints, vocabulary, or read-only reflexes.
4. **PENDING = publicly promised only** — an internal plan/discussion (the Commission Router
   class of error) NEVER becomes a public chip, badge, or slice.
5. **SEO-in-the-same-commit** — a slice that changes a page's reality updates that page's
   meta/OG/JSON-LD in the SAME commit (Invariant-vs-State applied to the SEO layer).
6. **HEARTBEAT COMPLETENESS INVARIANT** — any future contract or protocol wallet joins the
   activity inventory in the same slice that introduces it; the feed's completeness is an
   invariant, never a backlog.
7. **FOUNDER VOICE RULE** — when an act is the founder committing his own stake or signing
   with his own hand (liquidity adds, burns from his allocation, pauses, deployments,
   promotions), the sentence SAYS the founder. Skin in the game is the trust engine at this
   stage. Identity-blindness protects MEMBERS; it never hides the founder's public acts.
8. **VISIBILITY RULE** — lines carry what the chain publishes (amounts included); the ONE
   structural discipline: the server never emits a MEMBER's wallet address (no fabricated
   directory). Verify anchors expose the full transaction anyway. Never theatrical concealment.
